Founding AI Engineer (Contract-to-Hire)
Posted 08-May-2026 (EST)Remote, FL, USAContract
Email Me Similar Jobs
Email Me This Job
Tiger Tracks is hiring our first AI Engineer. We're one of the fastest-growing performance marketing agencies in the country, ranked #123 on the 2025 Inc. 5000 and founded by former Google leaders.

This is not a traditional engineering role. We're looking for a top-tier AI-native builder who can rapidly ship production-ready tools using platforms like Lovable, Cursor, Replit, or Bolt. Someone who can turn ideas into working products in 24 to 48 hours, not weeks.

You'll work directly with our co-founder Cliff Simmons (ex-Google) to build proprietary AI tools used internally and deployed across client accounts.

This is a contract-to-hire role with a path to becoming Head of AI.

WHAT YOU'LL BUILD

Landing page conversion analyzers with auto-fix capability
Creative analyzers for video ads
Vertical media buying co-pilots for Meta, Google, and TikTok APIs
Automated BD audit tool for prospect ad accounts
20+ more ideas waiting for someone who can ship them
We don't care which LLM sits underneath (Anthropic, Gemini, OpenAI, whatever ships best). The value is in the vertical specialization on top.

WHO YOU ARE

Technical background:

4+ years of full-stack development experience, including at least 1 year shipping LLM-powered features in production
Strong backend (Python, Node) and frontend (React) skills
Proven track record integrating complex external APIs AI-native builder:
Top 1% with Lovable, Replit, Cursor, or Bolt. You can ship a complex tool (multiple data sources, APIs, server hosting, LLM integration) in under a day. You've done this multiple times. - Public GitHub or portfolio with shipped projects we can play with
Comfortable moving from idea to working prototype in 24-48 hours
Mindset:

Thrive in greenfield environments
Shipping mindset: rapid prototyping over architecture debates
Genuinely excited about MarTech, AdTech, or performance marketing
STRUCTURE

Hourly contract, billed bi-weekly net-14
Monthly renewal based on output and mutual fit
Path to FTE and Head of AI for the right person
Remote, US timezones
WHAT WE'RE NOT LOOKING FOR

Engineers whose AI experience is mostly demos and tutorials
Generalists who'd rather think about architecture than ship code
Anyone who needs a PM, a designer, and a 6-week sprint to deliver a v1
Big-tech engineers looking for a calmer second act. This will be more intense, not less.
HOW TO APPLY

Send a short note, your GitHub or portfolio link, and one project you're proud of (with the live link if possible) to careers@tigertracks.ai. Tell us what you'd build first if we handed you Lovable, an OpenAI API key, and a Meta Ads account on day one.
