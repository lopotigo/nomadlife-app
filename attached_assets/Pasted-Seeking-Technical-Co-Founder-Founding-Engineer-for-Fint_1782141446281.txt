Summary
Hi,

I’m a solo founder from Sweden building Runda, a fintech startup focused on making saving money effortless.

The concept is simple:

Users connect their bank account through Open Banking, and every card purchase is automatically rounded up to the nearest whole amount. The spare change is then accumulated as savings.

Example:

* €4.60 purchase
* Rounded to €5.00
* €0.40 saved automatically

The goal is to help people build savings habits without changing their lifestyle.
